Usage

This design is good for anyone who wishes to display images or photos on the homepage of their site. I have provided the PSD files along with the source code and imagery. Have fun amending but please keep the backlink in the footer. Images are from sxc.hu, a great site for free imagery.

Author

My name is Callum Rimmer, and I run a web software company in Bath, England. We have created a simple content management system and hosting package for small companies and individuals called iFrame. iFrame is made to be as easy to use as possible whilst giving you the flexibility to design your site how you like. You can use any of the free designs on OSWD.org as an iFrame template.
